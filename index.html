Sorcer Striker

Sorcer Striker is an action genre videogame, specifically a Shoot’em up game.These games can form a genre themselves because they have specific characteristics, that is basically having multiple enemies which players will have to kill by shooting. It was released for arcades in Japan, in May 1993, by Able Corporation. The game is based on a story where players assume the role of one of the four heroes / characters and they’ll have to destroy and kill enemies to finally win the game.
